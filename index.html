About the Project

ReadyMyHome is a side project dedicated to building "permissionless" tools for responsible gun owners. We believe in privacy, security, and the right to self-defense.

Most digital tools track your data or hide behind paywalls. We build tools that run Client-Side, meaning your data never leaves your device.
